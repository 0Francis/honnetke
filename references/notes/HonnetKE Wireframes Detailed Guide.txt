HonnetKE Wireframes Detailed Guide

HonnetKE Brand Identity
Logo concept:
A honeycomb/hive icon with a clean modern wordmark. The contradiction is the hook — "Why is a hostel platform a hive?" — but it makes sense once you feel the community vibe.

Tagline options:
"Join the Hive"
"Find Your Cell" (as in honeycomb cell — also contradictory and fun)
"Where Students Nest"

Color Palette:
RoleColorHexPrimaryDeep Amber/Honey#E8900ASecondaryDark Charcoal#1E1E1EAccentWarm Cream/Off-white#FFF8ECSuccessSoft Green#2D9B5AErrorWarm Red#D94040
Typography:

Headings — bold, modern sans-serif (e.g. Plus Jakarta Sans)
Body — clean, readable (e.g. Inter)

Ideas!!
hornet animation 
Splash screen — hornet flying in to build the logo/hive. Perfect brand moment
Sign up page — subtle hornet flying across or sitting on the hive illustration. Adds life without distraction
404/error page — a confused or lost hornet. Fun and on-brand

SCREEN 1 — Splash Screen
Layout:

Full screen cream/off-white background
Center: HonnetKE hive logo + wordmark
Below logo: subtle loading spinner in amber
Tagline beneath: "Join the Hive"
No navigation, no buttons — pure brand moment

SCREEN 2 — Landing/Home Page (Guest)
Navbar (charcoal background):

Left: HonnetKE logo
Center: Home · Hostels
Right: Login · Sign Up (amber CTA button)

Hero Section:

Full width Kenyan rental/housing photo as background with a dark overlay
Bold headline over image: "Find Your Cell"
Subtext: "Discover affordable student hostels near your university"
Search bar with filters: Location · Price · Property Type · Gender · Room Type
Amber search button

Featured Hostels Section:

Section title: "Featured Hostels"
Grid of listing cards: image, title, location, price, property type, gender preference
Guest clicks card → redirected to login

Contact Section:

Hornets nest illustration on one side
Section title: "Found a Hidden Gem? Need Help?" or "Buzz Us"
Form fields: Name · Email · Phone · Subject · Message
Submit button → mailto: link to your support email
Warm amber/cream background to differentiate from hero

Footer:

Logo · Tagline · Basic links
