About this property

Sunrise Student Hostel is a well-maintained female-only hostel located just 5 minutes' walk from MMUST campus in Rongai. The property offers clean, comfortable single rooms with reliable water supply, 24-hour security, and a quiet study environment perfect for focused students.

Each room comes furnished with a bed, wardrobe, study desk, and chair. The hostel has a shared kitchen area, a common room with Wi-Fi, and on-site laundry facilities. The management is responsive and handles maintenance requests promptly.
